About Me

I am Kiran

A Data Engineer with around 6 years of experience designing large-scale ETL pipelines, building Snowflake and Azure data models, and optimizing SQL/PySpark workloads across enterprise and public-sector environments. With expertise in AWS, Azure, Snowflake, dbt, Databricks, Python, and SQL, I specialize in transforming raw data into reliable datasets that power dashboards, forecasting, and operational decision-making. Data infrastructure is constantly evolving, and so am I. Whether it's cloud data warehousing, real-time streaming architectures, or the latest data orchestration frameworks, I'm always looking ahead to build scalable and efficient data solutions.

I'm fascinated by how our brain influences motivation and decision-making, and I'm always looking for ways to apply these insights to both my personal and professional growth. I also love traveling—whether it's for new experiences or just to recharge, I find every trip brings new inspiration. And yes, when I'm not lifting weights, you might catch me experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, because balancing nutrition and flavors is a lot like balancing data—precision matters!
